Our Services

Real-time Beach Information

Service Overview:

Stay informed about the latest beach conditions, including water quality, tides, weather, and wave height, all updated in real-time.


Benefit:

Plan your day at the beach with confidence by having access to the most current environmental data.


How to Use:

Select your beach from the map or list, and view live updates on conditions to ensure safety and fun.


Water Quality Monitoring

Service Overview:

Receive updates on water safety, with overall water quality.


Benefit:

Ensure the water is safe for swimming.


How to Use:

View water quality reports for any beach by tapping on its location.


Weather and Tide Alerts

Service Overview:

Get real-time alerts for sudden weather changes, high tides, or other natural occurrences that may affect your beach plans.


Benefit:

Avoid unexpected weather disruptions or dangerous tide conditions by staying informed.


How to Use:

Weather and tide notifications is to receive instant alerts tailored to your location.


Safety and Lifeguard Services

Service Overview:

Access information about lifeguard availability and current safety warnings like riptides, dangerous currents, or marine life hazards.


Benefit:

Keep your beach day safe by knowing when it’s safe to swim or participate in beach activities.


How to Use:

Check the "Safety" tab for your beach to see lifeguard status, flag colors, and safety tips.


Reporting and Incident Management

Service Overview:

Report any issues or hazards on the beach.These reports are sent to the appropriate authorities for immediate action.


Benefit:

Contribute to the safety and cleanliness of your beach by reporting issues in real-time, ensuring quick responses.


How to Use:

You can track the status of your report within the app.
