About Us

Real-time Environmental Data

Water Quality Monitoring:

Display real-time information about the quality of water.


Tides and Waves:

Provide real-time tide and wave height data to help surfers, swimmers, and boaters make informed decisions.


Weather and Temperature:

Integrate real-time weather reports including air and water temperatures, wind speed, UV index, and forecast.


Beach Safety

Lifeguard Alerts:

Show information about lifeguard presence and current warnings for riptides, strong currents, or dangerous conditions.


Emergency Contacts:

Include direct access to local emergency services for quick help.


Beach Closure Alerts:

Notify users when beaches are closed due to unsafe conditions or environmental concerns.


Sustainability & Conservation Erosion Monitoring

Offer data or reports on beach erosion trends to raise awareness of environmental changes.


Community and User Engagement:

User Reports: Enable users to report hazards or issues like broken beach facilities, unsafe areas, or animal sightings.


Additional Features

Custom Alerts :

Let users customize alerts for specific conditions like high waves, low tides, or weather warnings.


Activity Recommendations:

Suggest suitable activities based on real-time conditions (e.g., surfing, swimming, beach sports).


This app can serve as a tool for local authorities, enhancing safety, environmental awareness, and the overall experience at the beach.
