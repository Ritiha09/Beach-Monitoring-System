Help & Support

Welcome to beach monitoring system, go-to source for real-time beach conditions, safety alerts, and environmental data.
For planning a day at the beach this app has everything you need.


How to Use the App:

Start by selecting location so we can show you the nearest beaches and real-time information.
Use menu to explore different features like beach reports, safety alerts, and sustainability efforts.


Environmental Data:

Water Quality:

Check water quality before you swim to avoid health risks.


Tides & Waves:

Use this feature to plan your beach activities like swimming or surfing.


Beach Safety :

Swim only in designated areas monitored by lifeguards for your safety.


Lifeguard Alerts:

Informs you of any active warnings such as strong currents or dangerous marine life.


If you need additional help or have a question that’s not covered,
feel free to contact our support team through the app's "Help & Support" section.
We’re here to assist you!
